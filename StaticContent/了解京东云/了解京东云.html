关于京东云

京东云（JDCloud.com）是京东集团旗下的云计算综合服务提供商，拥有全球领先的云计算技术和完整的服务平台。伴随着“互联网+”的迅速发展，京东云形成了从基础平台搭建、业务咨询规划，到业务平台建设及运营等全产业链的云生态格局。同时，京东云依托京东集团在云计算、大数据、物联网和移动互联网应用等多方面的长期业务实践和技术积淀，打造社会化的云服务平台，向全社会提供安全、专业、稳定、便捷的云计算专业服务。京东云旨在携手企业同步发展，创造全新的“互联网+”解决方案，帮助企业实现“互联网+”业务转型升级。

About JDCloud.com

JDCloud.com is the wholly-owned subsidy of JD Group, specialized in cloud computing and possessing world-leading technologies and services. With the rapid development of Internet+ in China, JDCloud.com has established a strong ecosystem that covers the entire value chain – from building infrastructure and advisory planning to setting up service platforms and everyday business operations. Through rich experience and strong technology in cloud computing, big data, IoT and mobile Internet inherited by JD Group over the years, JDCloud.com has been able to create a full-fledged cloud computing platform, providing secure, professional, stable and convenient services across all of society. Driven by the goal of win-win cooperation with business partners, JDCloud.com provides innovative Internet+ solutions that enable the transformation and upgrade of current business models toward new Internet+ technologies.
